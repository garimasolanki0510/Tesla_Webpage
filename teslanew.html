Powering a Cleaner Future




Tesla Electric is a retail electricity provider that allows you to power your home, vehicle and community
with sustainable electricity from Tesla. Our plans give you access to the energy you need when you need it
at a predictable cost — and reward you for supporting your community’s transition to sustainable energy.
To join, you must own a Tesla vehicle or Powerwall and live in an area of Texas that allows you to choose
your electricity provider.

Tesla Electric for Vehicles

Charge your Tesla vehicle at home overnight for a low fixed monthly fee, no matter how much you charge.
Our Home Charging Plan allows you to power your home with sustainable electricity all day while keeping
home charging costs for your vehicle predictable, so you can better control your energy bills. Learn More

One App, All Tesla

Tesla Electric for Powerwall

Earn credits toward your electricity bill by selling the extra energy stored in your Powerwall to the grid. If
you need to use electricity from the grid, it will always be matched with 100 percent Texas-generated clean
energy. If eligible, your Powerwall will also be connected to the Tesla Virtual Power Plant, allowing you to
share your stored energy with your community and help avoid blackouts when grid emergencies occur.

On average, Tesla Electric members have the potential to earn over 50 percent more in credits on their
electricity bills compared to similar plans. Learn More
